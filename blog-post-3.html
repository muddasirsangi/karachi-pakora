How to Keep Pakoras Crispy for Hours

The Science of Crispiness

Nothing beats the crunch of a perfectly fried pakora. Here's how to maintain that crispiness for hours.

Essential Tips:

  • Fry at 170-180°C: Correct temperature prevents oil absorption
  • Drain on Wire Rack: Never use paper towels—they trap steam
  • Store in Open Container: Allow air circulation
  • Reheat in Oven: 180°C for 5-7 minutes restores crispiness
