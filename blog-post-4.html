1. Mix Pakora Platter

Variety is key! Include vegetable, chicken, and paneer pakoras for everyone.

2. Aloo Pakora

Classic favorite that's filling and satisfying after a long fast.

3. Chicken Pakora

Protein-rich option perfect for breaking the fast.
