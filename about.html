About Pakora Palace

Serving Karachi's Finest Pakoras Since 2010

Our Story

Pakora Palace was born from a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of Karachi's street food culture into every home. In 2010, our founder, inspired by his grandmother's secret pakora recipe, opened a small shop in Gulshan-e-Iqbal with just one fryer and a passion for perfection.

What started as a humble venture has grown into Karachi's most beloved pakora destination. Every day, we hand-select fresh vegetables, source premium gram flour, and blend our signature spices to create pakoras that remind you of home, family gatherings, and rainy evenings.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across Karachi, but our commitment remains unchanged: crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and bursting with authentic flavors in every bite.
