Making Healthier Pakoras at Home

Love pakoras but watching your health? Here's how to make them healthier without sacrificing taste!

Healthy Cooking Methods

1. Air Fryer Method

Use 80% less oil! Spray pakoras lightly with oil and air fry at 180°C for 12-15 minutes.

Saves: 200+ calories per serving

2. Baked Pakoras

Brush with oil and bake at 200°C for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway.

Saves: 150+ calories per serving

3. Shallow Frying

Use minimal oil in a non-stick pan. Flip frequently for even cooking.

Saves: 100+ calories per serving

Healthier Ingredients

  • Add Vegetables: Spinach, carrots, bell peppers for extra nutrients
  • Use Whole Wheat: Mix 50% whole wheat flour with besan
  • Reduce Salt: Use herbs and spices for flavor instead
  • Choose Good Oil: Use olive or avocado oil for frying
