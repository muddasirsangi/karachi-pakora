5 Authentic Chutney Recipes

The perfect chutney can elevate your pakoras from good to unforgettable. Here are 5 authentic recipes.

1. Classic Mint Chutney

Ingredients: Fresh mint, coriander, green chilies, lemon juice, salt

Method: Blend all ingredients with a little water until smooth. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

2. Tamarind Chutney

Ingredients: Tamarind pulp, jaggery, cumin powder, red chili, salt

Method: Cook tamarind with jaggery and spices until thick. Cool and serve.

3. Garlic Chutney

Ingredients: Garlic cloves, red chilies, coconut, salt

Method: Grind all ingredients to a coarse paste. Perfect with chicken pakoras!

4. Yogurt Mint Dip

Ingredients: Yogurt, mint, cumin powder, salt, black pepper

Method: Mix all ingredients. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

5. Spicy Tomato Chutney

Ingredients: Tomatoes, onions, green chilies, ginger, spices

Method: Cook tomatoes with spices until thick. Blend and serve warm or cold.
