10 Spice Blends for Flavorful Pakoras

Discover traditional Pakistani spice combinations that will take your pakoras from good to absolutely irresistible.

The secret to memorable pakoras lies in the perfect blend of spices. Here are 10 authentic spice combinations used in Pakistani kitchens.

1. Classic Karachi Blend

Red chili powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala

Perfect for: Vegetable pakoras

2. Spicy Chicken Mix

Red chili, black pepper, ginger powder, garlic powder, chat masala

Perfect for: Chicken pakoras

3. Mild & Aromatic

Cumin, coriander, fennel seeds, dried fenugreek leaves

Perfect for: Paneer pakoras

4. Tangy Twist

Amchur (dried mango powder), chaat masala, cumin, red chili

Perfect for: Potato pakoras

5. Herbal Delight

Fresh coriander, mint, green chilies, cumin, ajwain

Perfect for: Spinach pakoras
